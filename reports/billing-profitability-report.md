# GymVault Pricing and Profit Report

## 1. Simple Answer

Ignore the current Rs 1, Rs 2, and Rs 3 plan prices in code. Those are test values only.

If your goal is to earn **Rs 1,00,000 profit per month after expenses**, my **recommended launch pricing** is:

| Plan | Recommended Monthly Price | Recommended Annual Price |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Basic | Rs 2,199 | Rs 21,990 |
| Growth | Rs 4,499 | Rs 44,990 |
| Pro | Rs 7,499 | Rs 74,990 |

Recommended annual pricing uses roughly **10 months of monthly pricing**, which means you give about **2 months free** for yearly customers.

With this pricing:

- If your sales mix is roughly **40% Basic, 40% Growth, 20% Pro**, your average revenue becomes about **Rs 4,179 per gym per month**.
- At that average, you need about **28 gyms** to cross **Rs 1 lakh monthly profit after fixed expenses**.
- To stay safe for MSG91, Hello inbound, payment gateway charges, refunds, and real-world leakage, your practical target should be **30 to 32 gyms**.

If you want a single direct recommendation, this is it:

- Basic: Rs 2,199/month
- Growth: Rs 4,499/month
- Pro: Rs 7,499/month
- Target: 30 to 32 active paying gyms

## 8. Important Business Insight

The key question is not only “what should the plan price be?”

The real question is:

**How many gyms do you realistically think you can sell?**

Because the answer changes the right price.

### If you think you can sell only 15 to 20 gyms

You should use the **Premium Model**.

### If you think you can sell around 30 gyms

You should use the **Balanced Model**.

### If you think you can sell 40 to 50+ gyms

You can use the **Scale Model**.
